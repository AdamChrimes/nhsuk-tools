Mood self-assessment quiz

When it comes to emotions, it can sometimes be hard to recognise or admit that we're not feeling 100%.

If you’re 16 or over, this mood self-assessment can:

  • help you better understand how you've been feeling over the last 2 weeks
  • point you in the right direction for helpful advice and information

It’s not meant to replace an appointment with a GP. If you’re struggling, it’s best to speak to someone.

If you’re under 16, the Young Minds website can offer help and support for young people’s mental health.
