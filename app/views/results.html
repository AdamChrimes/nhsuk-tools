Where to get medical help for a low mood or anxiety

Referring yourself for therapy

If you need more support, you can get free psychological therapies like cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) on the NHS.

You can refer yourself directly to a psychological therapies service without a referral from your GP.

Find a psychological therapies service in your area

Non-urgent advice: See a GP if:

  • you’ve had a low mood for more than 2 weeks
  • you’re struggling to cope with anxiety or a low mood
  • things you're trying yourself are not working
  • you would prefer to get a referral from your GP
